iToast Toastmasters Club is part of [Toastmasters International](https://toastmasters.org). We exist for people with a busy schedule in the weekdays but still wanting to be a part of this incredible family. Our metings are held on the second and fourth Saturdays of each month. Please check the [schedule]({{ site.url }}/schedule.html) for further details.
In a positive and friendly environment we learn from each other to advance our journey to success. And we are aided in this journey by the manuals provided by Toastmasters International, which we can peruse at our own pace.
